Why We Exist
Venues are evolving. Tech is advancing. The nature of Onsite Operations is changing. Everything. All at once. This is for the people that make it work now and in the future.

Event sites are undergoing radical transformation. New technologies are reshaping how we manage crowds, secure perimeters, move freight, and build temporary cities overnight. The industry needs best-in-class professionals to make this evolution functional and successful.

Onsite Ops Quarterly exists to capture this moment — to document, question, and elevate the craft of live event operations. All of this in the modern world? Yes. Because we read it all and give you our opinion so that you can read each article in 1 minute or less.

Every contribution is anonymous. The industry is big, but people talk. You won't see names here. Just facts that impact our jobs and our industry's future.
