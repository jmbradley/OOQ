## Notes

- **Week 1–2:** Let all alerts run, review daily, flag which ones are too noisy or too quiet.
- **Quiet alerts = content gold.** If nobody's writing about it, OOQ should be.
- **Noisy alerts:** Tighten the query or switch to "once a week" digest.
